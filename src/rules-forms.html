Marathon Series Rules Be sure you understand the rules before racing.

Team Challenge Rules The ultimate goal of the Team Challenge is to encourage and enhance mountain bike racing and riding in Arkansas. By providing friendly competition at the team level, we hope to promote the growth of existing teams and the creation of new ones. For teams to grow and develop, they must seek out new members. Being on a team is not required, but it adds to the fun of competition and allows riders to work together towards the ultimate goal of winning the team cup.
In addition to the overall Team Challenge Cup, the Future Champions Cup was created to specifically promote mountain biking among children. This competition is for those racers 14 and under. It is more important that ever to encourage life-time sports, such as cycling, to new generations. We hope the Future Champions Cup will provide our energetic and talented juniors with even more reason to ride their bikes.

Coordinators: Chad and Christopher ambcsteamchallenge@gmail.com
